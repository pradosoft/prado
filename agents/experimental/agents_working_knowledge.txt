
# Working Knowledge - Knowledge Retention Sub-Process Instructions
- The point of "Working Knowledge" is to make Coding Agents more efficient at their project tasks.
- The Working Knowledge directory, for saving planning, analysis, and accurate knowledge files, is "agents/working/"
- Scan (via "find agents/working/ -type f -name '*.md'") and parse relevant knowledge files relating to the task at hand
- Saved Working Knowledge file names must reflect its contents, for other agents to identify its contents
- Update the Working Knowledge files and their names (by moving files) as needed for efficient analysis, tasks, and scanning
- When Working Knowledge involves only a specific PRADO class, put the Working Knowledge files at "agents/working/classes/<relative_paths>/<class>.md" file location heuristic
- A primary point into the Working Knowledge is agents/working/INDEX.md and Working Knowledge directory scans; and generally should only be added to
- agents/working/INDEX.md must be compact with all the most important general Working Knowledge being added (and must contain links to other related files)
- Clean up any Working Knowledge files (and its directories) that are not needed or which are superseded
